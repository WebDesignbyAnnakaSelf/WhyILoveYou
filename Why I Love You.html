How It Started

The date was October 24, 2020. It only took a single letter for me to be aching for the next one. I wasn't looking for anything special, but great things happen when you least expect them, but when you need them the most. I didn't know how much I needed you untill I couldn't imagine a world without you.

My name is Henry, I’m a 16 year old kid from Georgia (the state not the country) and I’m currently living abroad in the Dominican Republic. What I recall first noting about this letter was the grammar and the way it was worded. Given your young age I was suprised by your maturity, so I replied.
